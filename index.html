Welcome to UNCP

Protecting and empowering vulnerable children, youth, and communities in Uganda.

Type: Community-Based Organization

Incorporated: Company Limited by Guarantee in Uganda

Founded: 2018

Mission: To protect and empower vulnerable children, youth, and communities.

Vision: A safe, inclusive, and resilient society where every child and vulnerable person thrives.

We deliver holistic, community-led interventions in child protection, GBV prevention, MHPSS, youth empowerment, inclusive education, and climate change adaptation.

We work across Uganda, especially in refugee and host communities in West Nile and other regions.
