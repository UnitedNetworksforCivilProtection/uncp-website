Welcome to United Networks for Civil Protection (UNCP)

We protect and empower vulnerable children, youth, and communities in Uganda through holistic, community-led programs.

1.1. Brief History of UNCP

United Networks for Civil Protection (UNCP) was founded in 2018 by two child protection specialists with extensive experience in humanitarian and refugee response programming in West Nile, Uganda. Drawing from years of frontline work with displaced and vulnerable populations, the founders established UNCP to provide sustained, community-led protection solutions beyond short-term emergency interventions. Today, both founders continue to serve in the child protection sector while leading UNCP's growth as a formally registered, community-based organization.

2. Mission and Vision

Mission: To protect and empower vulnerable children, youth, and communities through rights-based, locally led programs in child protection, gender-based violence prevention, mental health and psychosocial support, education, and climate-resilient livelihoods.

Vision: A safe, inclusive, and resilient society where every child and vulnerable person can thrive with dignity and equal opportunity.
