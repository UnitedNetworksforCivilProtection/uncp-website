Overview: United Networks for Civil Protection (UNCP) is a Community-Based Organization incorporated as a company limited by guarantee in Uganda. Established in 2018, UNCP is dedicated to protecting and empowering vulnerable children, youth, and communities.

Mission: To protect and empower vulnerable children, youth, and communities through rights-based, locally led programs.

Vision: A safe, inclusive, and resilient society where every child and vulnerable person can thrive with dignity and equal opportunity.

  • Child Protection
  • Gender-Based Violence (GBV) Prevention
  • Mental Health and Psychosocial Support (MHPSS)
  • Adolescent and Youth Empowerment
  • Climate Change Adaptation & Sustainable Livelihoods
  • Inclusive Education
