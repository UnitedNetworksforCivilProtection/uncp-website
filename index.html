About UNCP +

Overview

United Networks for Civil Protection (UNCP) is a community-based non-profit organization dedicated to protecting and empowering vulnerable children, youth, and communities, including forcibly displaced populations in Uganda.

History

Founded in 2018 by child protection specialists, UNCP has implemented projects in child protection, youth empowerment, GBV prevention, MHPSS, inclusive education, climate resilience, and livelihoods support.

Mission

To ensure safe, inclusive, and resilient communities through protection, empowerment, and social support initiatives.

Vision

A society where children, youth, and vulnerable groups live free from harm, access opportunities equally, and actively participate in community development.

What We Do +
  • Child Protection: Case management, safeguarding, family support, and community engagement.
  • GBV Prevention: Awareness, prevention, response, and survivor support programs.
  • MHPSS: Counseling, psychosocial interventions, and referrals.
  • Youth Empowerment: Skills development, mentorship, and leadership programs.
  • Climate & Livelihoods: Sustainable agriculture, climate resilience, and income-generating activities.
  • Inclusive Education: Support for children with disabilities and access to quality learning opportunities.
