About UNCP

Organization Overview

United Networks for Civil Protection (UNCP) is a community-based organization (CBO) founded in 2018 in Arua, Uganda. Legal status: Non-profit, non-political, non-religious. UNCP works with vulnerable children, youth, and communities in refugee and host settings, delivering holistic, community-led protection and empowerment interventions.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To protect and empower vulnerable children, youth, and communities through rights-based, locally led programs in child protection, gender-based violence prevention, mental health and psychosocial support, education, and climate-resilient livelihoods.

Vision: A safe, inclusive, and resilient society where every child and vulnerable person can thrive with dignity and equal opportunity.
