Our History

UNCP was founded in 2018 by two child protection specialists with extensive experience in humanitarian and refugee response programming in West Nile, Uganda. Drawing from years of frontline work with displaced and vulnerable populations, the founders established UNCP to provide sustained, community-led protection solutions beyond the scope of short-term emergency interventions.

Mission

To protect and empower vulnerable children, youth, and communities through rights-based, locally led programs in child protection, gender-based violence prevention, mental health and psychosocial support, education, and climate-resilient livelihoods.

Vision

A safe, inclusive, and resilient society where every child and vulnerable person can thrive with dignity and equal opportunity.
