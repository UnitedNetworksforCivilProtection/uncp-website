About Us

United Networks for Civil Protection (UNCP) is a community-based organization founded in 2018 and headquartered in Arua City, Uganda. We are legally registered as a non-profit, non-political, and non-religious organization.

Mission

To protect and empower vulnerable children, youth, and communities through rights-based, locally led programs in child protection, GBV prevention and response, mental health and psychosocial support, education, and climate-resilient livelihoods.

Vision

A safe, inclusive, and resilient society where every child and vulnerable person can thrive with dignity and equal opportunity.

Our Thematic Areas

  • Child Protection
  • Gender-Based Violence (GBV) Prevention and Response
  • Mental Health and Psychosocial Support (MHPSS)
  • Adolescent and Youth Employment
  • Climate Change Adaptation & Sustainable Livelihoods
  • Inclusive Education
