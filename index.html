About UNCP

Overview

United Networks for Civil Protection (UNCP) is a community-based organization founded in 2018 focused on child protection, youth empowerment, GBV prevention, mental health, education, and climate-resilient livelihoods.

Our Partners

We collaborate with local communities, government agencies, NGOs, and international donors to maximize impact.

Governance & Oversight

UNCP is legally registered as a non-profit, non-political, non-religious CBO with a diverse Board of Directors ensuring accountability.
