Welcome to UNCP

Founded in 2018, United Networks for Civil Protection (UNCP) is a community-based, non-profit, non-political, and non-religious organization headquartered in Arua City, Uganda. We work to protect and empower vulnerable children, youth, and communities through rights-based, locally led programs.

Vision

A safe, inclusive, and resilient society where every child and vulnerable person can thrive with dignity and equal opportunity.

Mission

To protect and empower vulnerable children, youth and communities through rights-based, locally led programs in child protection, GBV prevention and response, mental health and psychosocial support, education and climate-resilient livelihoods.

Thematic Areas

  • Child Protection
  • GBV Prevention and Response
  • Mental Health and Psychosocial Support
  • Adolescent and Youth Employment
  • Climate Change Adaptation and Sustainable Livelihoods
  • Inclusive Education
