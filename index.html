About UNCP

Founded in 2018, UNCP is a community-based non-profit organization incorporated as a company limited by guarantee in Uganda. Our mission is to protect and empower vulnerable children, youth, and communities. We envision a safe, inclusive, and resilient society.

Where We Work

We operate across Uganda, particularly in refugee-hosting and vulnerable districts in West Nile and Northern regions.

What We Do

Protecting vulnerable children through community-based interventions and safeguarding measures.

Preventing gender-based violence and supporting survivors through holistic interventions.

Empowering communities with sustainable livelihoods while building resilience to climate change impacts.

Promoting access to quality education for all children, including marginalized groups.

Providing mental health and psychosocial services to children, youth, and communities affected by adversity.

Equipping youth with life skills and leadership training to foster personal and community development.
