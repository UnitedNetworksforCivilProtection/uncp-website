About UNCP +

Overview

United Networks for Civil Protection (UNCP) is a community-based organization focused on child protection, youth empowerment, and support to vulnerable populations including refugees and displaced communities.

History

Founded in 2018 by child protection specialists with extensive experience in humanitarian programs, UNCP has grown into a leading organization supporting communities in Uganda.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Protect and empower children, youth, and communities. Vision: A resilient and inclusive society where vulnerable populations thrive.
