About UNCP

Overview

United Networks for Civil Protection (UNCP) is a humanitarian organization dedicated to safeguarding and empowering vulnerable communities and populations.

History

Founded in 2018 by child protection specialists, UNCP has grown to serve thousands of children, youth, and displaced communities.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To protect and empower vulnerable populations, providing them with the resources, care, and advocacy they need to thrive.

Vision: A world where every child, youth, and community can live safely, with dignity and opportunity, even in the face of displacement and crisis.
