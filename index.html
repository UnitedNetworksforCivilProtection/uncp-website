Welcome to UNCP

United Networks for Civil Protection (UNCP) is a non-profit organization committed to strengthening community resilience and promoting civil protection initiatives across Uganda. Our mission is to empower communities to prevent, prepare for, and respond to emergencies through awareness, advocacy, and collaboration.
