Who We Are

We are a community-based non-profit organization committed to fostering inclusive and resilient communities across Uganda. Founded in 2018, UNCP works to protect and empower vulnerable children, youth, and communities for a safer society.

What We Do

  • Child Protection: Community-based initiatives to protect children from abuse and exploitation.
  • GBV Prevention & Response: Preventing and responding to gender-based violence.
  • Sustainable Livelihoods & Climate Change Resilience: Supporting community livelihoods while promoting climate resilience.
  • Inclusive Education: Ensuring access to quality education for all children.
  • MHPSS: Providing mental health and psychosocial support.
  • Youth & Adolescents Empowerment: Life skills and youth development programs.
